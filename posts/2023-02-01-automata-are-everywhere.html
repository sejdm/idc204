Finite State Automata are everywhere

1 February, 2023 (Wednesday)

<<

 Last modified: 12:52 PM - 1 February, 2023

PDF version

It may seem that finite state automata are very artificial and specific. But they are equivalent to many known computational devices and therefore studying their limitations tells you about the limitations of many such devices.

Your desktop, laptop, or smartphone are all practically finite state automata.
